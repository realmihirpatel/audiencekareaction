❤️ Birthday Chapter 3

When I Started Falling For You

I never planned it. It just happened...

❤️ When Things Started Changing

As the days passed, something started changing inside me.

At first, I couldn't even understand what it was.

Was it friendship? Was it something more? Was it romantic? Was it bromantic? Or was it just me overthinking everything?

Honestly... I had no idea.

All I knew was that I started caring about you more than I cared about most people.

Your happiness mattered to me. Your bad days affected me. Your achievements made me genuinely happy.

And somehow, without realizing it, I became a little possessive too.

Not because I wanted to control you. But because you had become important to me. Very important.

I always wanted to know if you were okay. I always wanted to be there when you needed someone.

And if there was anything I could do to make you smile, I would probably do it without thinking twice.

And yes... there is something I need to confess.

I get jealous.

Way more than I pretend to.

Whenever some random guy starts falling for you, a tiny voice inside my head immediately goes:

"HELLOOO??? 😭
Excuse me?
Can we talk about this for a second?"

Sometimes I genuinely wonder...

Do you really not notice me getting jealous?

Because I thought I was making it pretty obvious.

I mean... why are you out there making random boys fall for you when I'm already trying my best to be your favourite person? ❤️

But even with all the teasing, all the jealousy, all the overthinking, and all these emotions...

The truth is simple.

I care about you. More than I expected to. More than I planned to. And definitely more than I can put into words.

Somewhere between random messages, late-night conversations, silly arguments, stupid jokes, and countless memories...

I fell for you. ❤️
