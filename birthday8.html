A Letter For You

Dear My Favourite Person ❤️,

If you've reached this page, then you've already travelled through our story. You've seen how we met, how I slowly fell for you, the memories we've created, the way you changed my life, and the dreams I have for our future.

But there are still things I don't say often enough.

Thank you.

Thank you for staying. Thank you for listening. Thank you for making me smile on days when I didn't even feel like smiling.

Back in 2022, I was just a guy in a random group chat. You were just a random girl in that same group chat.

Neither of us knew that years later we'd be standing here, with me writing an entire website dedicated to you.

Life is funny like that.

One random invite. One random conversation. One random message.

And suddenly someone becomes one of the most important people in your life.

Sometimes I wonder what would've happened if that person had never added me to your GC.

Would we have met? Would we have talked? Would I have found someone who understands me the way you do?

Honestly... I don't know.

But I'm grateful that life chose this path. Because it gave me you.

You don't have to be perfect. You never did.

I love your smile. I love your laugh. I love your weirdness. I love your chaos. I love the way you care.

I love the little things about you that most people probably don't even notice.

And if there's one thing I hope you remember after reading this...

It's that you are loved. More than you realize. More than you think. And definitely more than I can explain in words.

Happy Birthday, my favourite person. Thank you for being the best chapter of my story. ❤️

With Love,
