The Day Everything Changed

The beginning of a story I never saw coming...

💌 Our Story

Back in 2022, I was alone.

Life was moving on, days were passing, and I never imagined that someone would enter my life and become this important to me.

Then one random day, something happened that completely changed everything.

Someone added me to a group chat.

And there you were.

Just a random girl in a random GC.

At first, I never thought much about it. We talked, joked around, and yes... I used to flirt with you sometimes.

To me, it was just another conversation. Just another person on the internet.

But little did I know, life was quietly writing the most beautiful chapter of my story.

Days became weeks. Weeks became months.

Somehow your messages became the ones I looked forward to the most.

Your smile became my favourite sight. Your happiness started mattering to me. Your presence became a part of my daily life.

I never knew that one day I would be so deeply in love with you.

Looking back now, I am thankful to that random person who added me to your GC.

Because if that never happened, our paths might never have crossed.

And I would never have met the girl who changed my life forever.

Somewhere between random messages, silly conversations, late-night chats, and countless memories... I fell in love with you. ❤️
